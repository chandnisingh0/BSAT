Understanding Reliability Ratings

The rating status is calculated automatically based on the volume and severity of unresolved issues found within the statement.

CLEAN
Zero Inconsistencies

No Critical, High, or Medium issues detected. The transactions match expected balance rules and narration criteria.

ACCEPTABLE
Minor Warnings

No Critical or High issues found. Some Medium issues exist (e.g. potential near-duplicate transactions or short descriptions).

QUALIFIED
Needs Investigation

Contains High issues (like transaction gaps, ledger balance mismatches, or dual entries) that need analyst resolution.

UNRELIABLE
Critical Errors

Contains Critical issues (e.g. negative transaction amounts, missing headers, or blank fields in large values) that require immediate action.
